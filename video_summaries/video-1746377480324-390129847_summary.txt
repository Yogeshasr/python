The tutorial explains how to create a new timesheet as an employee using the Easy Time application. The steps are:

1. Log in with your username and password.
2. Click on "New Time Sheet" from the onboarding screen.
3. Select the time sheet period and click "New Line".
4. Fill in details such as project, activity, hours, and supervisor.
5. Save each row of details.
6. Submit the timesheet after reviewing it.
7. Confirm submission by selecting "Self" in the pop-up review.

The tutorial aims to guide employees through the process of creating and submitting a new timesheet using the Easy Time application.
