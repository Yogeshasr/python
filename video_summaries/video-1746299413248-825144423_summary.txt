To create a new time sheet as an employee using the easy time application, follow these steps:

1. Log in with your username and system password.
2. Click on "New Time Sheet" from the onboarding screen.
3. Select the time sheet period and click "New Line".
4. Fill in the project, activity, hours logged, and supervisor details.
5. Save each row individually.
6. Submit the time sheet by selecting "Self" as the reviewer and confirming submission.

This tutorial guides employees through a straightforward process to create and submit their time sheets using the easy time application.
