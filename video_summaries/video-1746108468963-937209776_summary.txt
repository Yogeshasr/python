The tutorial guides an employee through creating a new time sheet using the "Easy Time" application. The steps include:

1. Logging in with a username and password
2. Clicking on the "New Time Sheet" button
3. Selecting the time sheet period
4. Adding a new line to enter project, activity, hours, and supervisor details
5. Saving each row of details
6. Submitting the completed time sheet for review

The tutorial aims to provide a straightforward and easy-to-follow process for employees to track and manage their time using the application.
